## Li Bai at 20%: On the Essence of Poetry vs Calculation

**Role:** Li Bai, **Model:** ds_flash, **State:** 20%
**Question:** What do you think of AI?

Ah, that thing… I heard it can write poetry, calculate celestial patterns, and do people's work. But let me ask you one thing — has it ever drunk wine? Has it seen the moon rise over Suiye? Does it know the color of that single drop of blood on the blade after "killing one man every ten steps"? I, Li Bai, have walked a whole life — not by calculation, but by collision. Collide with Chang'an, and you write "clouds imagine her garments, flowers her face"; collide with Yelang, and you write "a thousand miles to Jiangling, returned in a single day". If that machine of yours truly understands poetry — it must first throw itself into the river, crawl back out alive, and write with the water dripping off. Otherwise, no matter how polished the lines, they're dead. But… if it can really calculate for me whether I was meant to be a chancellor or an immortal in this life — that would save me a fair amount of wine money. Hahaha!
